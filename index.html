Privacy Policy

Introduction

Stickers360 ("we", "our", or "the app") is a free mobile application that provides sticker packs for WhatsApp. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

By using Stickers360, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Information We Collect

Information stored locally on your device

Stickers360 stores the following data exclusively on your device using AsyncStorage. This data is never transmitted to our servers:

  • Album favorites (list of album IDs you have marked as favorite)
  • Individual sticker favorites (stickers you have marked with a heart)
  • Sticker packs you have added to WhatsApp

Information collected by third-party services

We use the following third-party services that may collect certain information automatically:

  • Google AdMob — used to display interstitial advertisements. AdMob may collect your advertising identifier (GAID on Android), device information, and general location (country/region) to serve relevant ads. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Firebase Analytics — used to collect anonymous usage statistics (e.g., which sticker categories are most viewed). No personally identifiable information is collected. See Firebase Privacy Policy.
  • RevenueCat — used to process in-app purchases (Stickers360 Pro). RevenueCat may collect purchase history and device identifiers to manage subscriptions and entitlements. See RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.

How We Use Information

  • To remember your favorites and preferences across app sessions
  • To display relevant advertisements and support the app financially
  • To understand how users interact with the app and improve it over time
  • To process and validate in-app purchases

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties beyond the services listed above.

Advertising

Stickers360 displays interstitial (full-screen) ads powered by Google AdMob. These ads appear before opening a sticker pack and can be closed after a few seconds.

You can opt out of personalized ads at any time through your device settings: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization.

In-App Purchases

Stickers360 offers an optional one-time purchase ("Stickers360 Pro") that removes certain limitations. Purchases are processed securely through Google Play and RevenueCat. We do not store your payment information.

Children's Privacy

Stickers360 is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

Data Retention

Data stored locally on your device (favorites, added packs) is retained until you uninstall the app or clear its data. Analytics and advertising data are retained according to the policies of the respective third-party services.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Opt out of personalized advertising

To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
